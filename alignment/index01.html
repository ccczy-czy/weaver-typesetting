This book examines the mutating technological infrastructures of typesetting built on the Latin alphabet, while taking a broad view to examine non-Western typographic systems and histories, suggesting alterna- tive approaches and avenues for more research. It favors specificity over completeness, and is a snapshot of an ongoing process of research organized around our inter- ests. This book was assembled by Faye Arranz, Victoria Beyrard, Anna Blyumenkrants, Han Bao, Jieun Han, Grace Huang, Chris Kim, Rosie Mai, Yingjie Wang, Shiyao Wu, Tong Wu, Xin-Yi Isabelle Yu, and Yixiao Zhao, in the context of T y p e s e t t i n g, a course initiated by Andrew LeClair at Parsons School of Design, The New School, in New York.
